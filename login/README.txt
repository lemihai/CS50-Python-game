Controls

w - up
d - right
s - down
a - left

path : 
1. Move the dog to the diamond first until the window opens to enter your name
2. enter your name
3. go to the emerald until the terminal prompts to press g
4. press g
5. start collecting carrots
6. collect at least 10
7. exit the program by moving the dog to the fire. 
8. the terminal will print the name, the password and the distance travelled on the map.
